# **Content Database: Experimental Tech (Campaign Relics)**

## **Overview**

Experimental Tech represents highly illegal, experimental, or alien hardware scavenged during your flight from the Hegemony.

* **Acquisition:** Awarded by defeating \[Elite\] nodes, resolving difficult \[Event\] nodes, or occasionally purchased at a Black Market \[Haven\] for exorbitant RP costs.  
* **Effect:** Unless stated otherwise, these are **Fleet-Wide Passive Buffs**. As soon as they are acquired, they bend or break the core rules of the game for all players simultaneously.  
* **Stacking:** The fleet can carry an unlimited amount of Experimental Tech.

## **1\. Tactical & Ordnance Tech (Combat)**

*These artifacts fundamentally alter how the fleet rolls dice and deals damage.*

* **Plasma Accelerators**  
  * *Effect:* The maximum range of all non-\[Ordnance\] weapon modules equipped by the fleet is increased by 1 Hex.  
  * *Flavor:* "We bypassed the safety regulators. The coils will melt eventually, but today, we outrange them."  
* **Void-Glass Lenses**  
  * *Effect:* Whenever an officer rolls their Tactical Skill Die in a Volley Pool, a result of exactly 1 may be rerolled once.  
  * *Flavor:* "Perfectly focuses the beam. Just don't look directly at the emitter when it fires."  
* **Kinetic Siphon**  
  * *Effect:* Whenever a player ship lands the killing blow on an Enemy Capital Ship, that player immediately restores 1 Shield point to all four of their ship's sectors.  
  * *Flavor:* "It converts the enemy's reactor death-throe into a localized electromagnetic surge."  
* **Tachyon Targeting Matrix (Rare)**  
  * *Effect:* Once per combat scenario, during Phase 3, the War Council may declare a "Tachyon Strike." Choose one standard Hit just rolled in any Volley Pool and manually convert it into a Critical Hit (which then explodes as normal).  
  * *Flavor:* "It calculates the firing solution before the enemy even knows they're in combat."

## **2\. Engineering & Defensive Tech (Survivability)**

*Artifacts that keep the defector fleet from being reduced to scrap metal.*

* **Inertial Dampeners**  
  * *Effect:* The first time *each* player ship would take Collision/Ramming damage in a scenario, completely negate that damage.  
  * *Flavor:* "Brace for impact... actually, never mind. We barely felt it."  
* **Hard-Light Plating**  
  * *Effect:* The very first point of Hull damage taken by each player ship during a combat scenario is reduced to 0\.  
  * *Flavor:* "An experimental Hegemony prototype. It shatters after one hit, but it absorbs the kinetic shock perfectly."  
* **Recycled Coolant**  
  * *Effect:* Once per round, any single Officer in the fleet may ignore the \+1 Fatigue Penalty when activating a node they have already used this round.  
  * *Flavor:* "It smells like ozone and bleach, but it keeps the consoles from catching fire."  
* **Auto-Doc Override (Consumable)**  
  * *Effect:* The first time any Bridge Officer in the fleet would gain a permanent Trauma Trait, they do not. The trauma is negated, and this relic is destroyed and removed from the fleet's inventory.  
  * *Flavor:* "A highly illegal cocktail of combat stims and memory-wipes. Only good for one dose."

## **3\. Command & Logistics (Economy & Map)**

*Artifacts that manipulate Fleet Favor (FF), Requisition Points (RP), and Sector Map navigation.*

* **The Admiral's Black-Box**  
  * *Effect:* The fleet starts every combat scenario with \+1 Fleet Favor (FF) already on the track.  
  * *Flavor:* "We spoofed their IFF codes. High Command thinks we're just a loyal patrol fleet... for the first three minutes, anyway."  
* **Salvager Drones**  
  * *Effect:* The fleet earns a bonus \+5 RP at the end of the mission for every Hegemony Small Craft (Corvette or Interceptor Wing) destroyed during the combat.  
  * *Flavor:* "Release the swarm. Strip them down to the bolts before they even cool."  
* **Smuggler's Manifest**  
  * *Effect:* At all \[Haven\] Drydock nodes, the "Hull Patch" service costs 3 RP instead of 5 RP.  
  * *Flavor:* "Turns out, if you know the right black market frequencies, titanium plating is dirt cheap."  
* **Hegemony Encryption Key (Rare)**  
  * *Effect:* Upon entering a new Sector, immediately reveal the specific details of all \[Elite\] and \[Event\] nodes on the Sector Map.  
  * *Flavor:* "We have their deployment schedules. We know exactly where the traps are set."

## **4\. Crew & Psychology (Stress Management)**

*Artifacts that keep your bridge crew from cracking under the pressure of deep space warfare.*

* **Combat Stim-Injectors**  
  * *Effect:* Every Bridge Officer in the fleet increases their Maximum Stress Limit by \+1.  
  * *Flavor:* "Legal? No. Effective? Very. Just ignore the twitching."  
* **Astro-Caf Synthesizer**  
  * *Effect:* At the end of Phase 4, if a player's ship took absolutely no Hull damage that round, that player may remove 1 Stress from any of their Bridge Officers.  
  * *Flavor:* "It tastes like battery acid, but it's the only thing keeping the graveyard shift awake."  
* **Neural Link Uplink (Rare)**  
  * *Effect:* Whenever an officer resolves an action that costs 3 or more Stress (including Fatigue Penalties), immediately gain 1 Command Token (CT) into that player's unspent pool.  
  * *Flavor:* "The interface translates sheer panic into raw processing power."
