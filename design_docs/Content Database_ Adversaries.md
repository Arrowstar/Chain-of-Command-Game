* **Hegemony Hunter-Killer (Frigate)**  
  * *Size:* Small | *Hull:* 6 | *Shields:* 3 (Fore Sector Only)  
  * *Armor Die:* D4 | *Speed:* 3 Hexes | *Base Evasion:* 6  
  * *Weapon Volley:* 2x D6 (Range 1-3)  
  * *AI Tag (Aggressive):* Closes distance rapidly. Attempts to enter the target's weakest shield arc.  
* **Hegemony Monitor (Artillery Cruiser)**  
  * *Size:* Medium | *Hull:* 12 | *Shields:* 5 (All Sectors)  
  * *Armor Die:* D4 | *Speed:* 1 Hex | *Base Evasion:* 4  
  * *Weapon Volley:* 1x D12, 2x D8 (Range 4-8)  
  * *AI Tag (Sniper):* Moves away from the player to maintain maximum firing range. Will hide behind asteroids if closer than Range 4\.  
* **Hegemony Carrier (Capital Ship)**  
  * *Size:* Large | *Hull:* 18 | *Shields:* 6 (All Sectors)  
  * *Armor Die:* D6 | *Speed:* 1 Hex | *Base Evasion:* 3  
  * *Weapon Volley:* 2x D8 (Range 1-4)  
  * *AI Tag (Support):* Moves away from combat. Spawns 2 Strike Fighter tokens every round during Step 3\.  
* **Hegemony Strike Fighter (Small Craft)**  
  * *Size:* Small | *Hull:* 1 (No Shields/Armor) | *Speed:* 4 Hexes | *Base Evasion:* 8  
  * *Weapon Volley:* 3x D4 (Range 1\)

## **2\. Hegemony AI Tactic Deck**

*Drawn at the start of Phase 1\. Modifies all Enemy AI for the round.*

* **Card: Pincer Movement**  
  * *Effect:* All AI ships gain \+1 Hex movement. Targeting priority shifts to prioritize Player Ships on the flanks or rear of the player formation.  
* **Card: Overwhelming Firepower**  
  * *Effect:* AI ships do not move this round. All AI weapons gain \+1 Skill Die (add 1x D6 to all enemy Volley Pools).  
* **Card: Shields to Maximum**  
  * *Effect:* Restore 3 Shield points to all enemy sectors. Player Volley dice rolls of 8 or higher are required to cause Critical Hits this round.  
* **Card: Target the Bridge**  
  * *Effect:* All AI ships prioritize the Player Ship with the highest remaining Hull, ignoring standard proximity targeting rules.  
* **Card: Electronic Jamming**  
  * *Effect:* Player ships cannot use "Sensors" actions this round.
