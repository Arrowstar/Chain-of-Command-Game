# **Content Database: Bridge Crew**

## **1\. Officer Roster**

*Players select one officer per station before a campaign/mission.*

### **Helm (Navigation & Evasion)**

* **Lt. "Slick" Jones**  
  * *Trait (Hotshot):* Movement through hazardous terrain only causes damage on a D6 roll of 1\.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 4  
* **Lt. Cmdr. T'Lari**  
  * *Trait (Methodical):* "Rotate" actions cost 0 Stress once per round.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 6  
* **Ensign "Jitters" Kael**  
  * *Trait (Paranoia):* "Evasive Pattern" actions grant \+3 to Base Evasion instead of \+2, but cost \+1 additional Stress to perform.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 3 (Very easily stressed, but highly evasive).  
* **Crewman 1st Class Orlov**  
  * *Trait (Lead Foot):* "Adjust Speed" actions cost 0 Stress if you are increasing your speed.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 5

### **Tactical (Weapons)**

* **Lt. Vane**  
  * *Trait (Bloodlust):* \+1 Attack die (D6) when targeting a ship that is below 50% Hull.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 5  
* **Ensign Rutherford**  
  * *Trait (By The Book):* Never triggers "Fumbles" when Stress is maxed, but cannot perform any actions while at max Stress.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 5  
* **Gunner Sgt. "Boomer" Hayes**  
  * *Trait (Heavy Loader):* The "Load Ordinance" action costs 0 CT (it still costs 1 Stress).  
  * *Stress Limit:* 6  
* **Sub-Commander Vex**  
  * *Trait (Surgical Strike):* Once per round, you may manually change one of your Standard Hits in the Volley Pool into a Critical Hit.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 4

### **Engineering (Damage Control & Shields)**

* **Chief O'Bannon**  
  * *Trait (Miracle Worker):* Once per game, repair 1 Critical Damage effect instantly without spending a CT or rolling a die.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 7  
* **"Sparky" (AI Drone)**  
  * *Trait (Synth-Logic):* Immune to all Stress mechanics and Fumbles. However, the "Damage Control" action costs 3 CT instead of 2\.  
  * *Stress Limit:* N/A  
* **Specialist Dr. Aris**  
  * *Trait (Deflector Specialist):* "Reinforce Shields" restores 3 points instead of 2, but can only be applied to the Fore or Aft shield sectors.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 5  
* **Engineer "Scorch" Malikov**  
  * *Trait (Redliner):* "Reroute Power" grants \+3 Command Tokens instead of \+2, but immediately deals 1 unblockable Hull damage to your ship.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 6

### **Sensors (Electronic Warfare & Intel)**

* **Lt. Cmdr. Vance**  
  * *Trait (Eagle Eye):* "Target Lock" actions grant \+2 to the attacker's Volley Pool instead of \+1.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 5  
* **Operative Xel**  
  * *Trait (Hacker):* "Cyber-Warfare" actions cost 1 less CT (costs 1 CT instead of 2).  
  * *Stress Limit:* 4  
* **Lt. "Chatter" Singh**  
  * *Trait (Fleet Comms):* As long as Singh has 0 Stress, any Allied ships within 2 hexes of your ship gain \+1 to their Base Evasion.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 5  
* **Chief Petty Officer D'Vesh**  
  * *Trait (Ghost Maker):* You may spend 1 CT during Phase 1 to completely cancel the Hegemony AI Tactic Card as it is revealed. If you do, D'Vesh immediately takes 3 Stress.  
  * *Stress Limit:* 6

## **2\. Standard Action Nodes (Worker Placement)**

*These are the baseline nodes found on every player's Captain's Dashboard. Assigning a Command Token (CT) here activates the effect.*

| Station | Action Name | CT Cost | Stress | Base Effect |
| :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **Helm** | Adjust Speed | 1 | 0 | Increase or decrease the ship's Current Speed by 1 (Minimum 0, up to Chassis Max Speed). Applies to the NEXT round's drift. |
| **Helm** | Rotate | 1 | 1 | Rotate the ship 60 degrees (one face) in either direction. |
| **Helm** | Evasive Pattern | 2 | 2 | \+2 Base Evasion (TN) for this round. |
| **Tactical** | Fire Primary | 1 | 1 | Select a weapon module and roll its Volley Pool at a valid target. |
| **Tactical** | Load Ordinance | 1 | 0 | Prime heavy weapons (Torpedoes/Missiles) to be fired next turn. |
| **Engineering** | Reinforce Shields | 1 | 1 | Restore 2 Shield points to any single Shield Sector. |
| **Engineering** | Damage Control | 2 | 2 | Repair 1 Hull point or attempt to clear a Critical Damage card (roll 4+ on D6). |
| **Engineering** | Reroute Power | 1 | 3 | Gain \+2 Command Tokens for this round. |
| **Sensors** | Target Lock | 1 | 0 | Decrease TN by 2 for one allied attack against a specific target this round. |
| **Sensors** | Cyber-Warfare | 2 | 2 | Disable one enemy Shield Sector (reduce to 0\) for the current round. |
