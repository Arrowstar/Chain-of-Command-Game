## **2\. Scenario 1: "Ambush at Kaelen-IV"**

* **Narrative:** "We jumped out of hyperspace right into a Hegemony listening post. The fleet is scattered. Form up, survive the initial barrage, and destroy their comms array before they call for a Dreadnought."  
* **Player Count:** 2 to 4 Players.  
* **Map Layout:** \* Dense Asteroids in the center.  
  * Player ships spawn scattered on the outer edges.  
  * Hegemony Comms Array (Objective Marker) is heavily shielded in the dead center of the asteroids.  
* **Enemy Spawns:** \* 3x Hegemony Hunter-Killers (Frigates) patrolling the inner asteroid ring.  
  * 2x Hegemony Strike Fighter Swarms spawn on Round 2\.  
* **Starting Directive:** *Survive and Regroup.* "End Round 2 with all surviving Player Ships within 2 hexes of each other." (Reward: \+3 FF).  
* **Victory Condition:** Destroy the Hegemony Comms Array (Hull 15, Shields 4 per sector) before the end of Round 8\.  
* **Defeat Condition:** Round 8 ends (A Hegemony Dreadnought arrives and annihilates the sector), OR standard Fleet Defeat conditions.
