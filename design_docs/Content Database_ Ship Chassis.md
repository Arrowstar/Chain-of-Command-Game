# **Content Database: Ship Chassis**

## **1\. Ship Chassis (Hulls)**

Players select one of these hulls at the start of a campaign or scenario. Each chassis defines the ship's base stats and available module slots.  
**Key Terms:**

* **Size:** Determines when the ship activates during Phase 3 (Small, Medium, or Large).  
* **Base Evasion:** The base Target Number (TN) enemies must roll to hit you.  
* **Armor Die:** Rolled to mitigate incoming Hull damage after shields fall.  
* **CT Gen:** The Base Command Tokens generated during Phase 1\.  
* **Max Speed:** The maximum momentum/drift value the ship can achieve.  
* **RP Cost:** The Requisition Point cost to purchase this hull at a Drydock.

### **1\. Vanguard-Class Light Cruiser**

*The workhorse of the Hegemony defector fleet. A perfectly balanced ship for any scenario.*

* **Size:** Medium | **Base Hull:** 12 | **Shields:** 4 per sector | **Armor Die:** D4  
* **CT Gen:** 5 | **Base Evasion:** 5 | **Max Speed:** 3 | **RP Cost:** 50  
* **Slots:** 2 Weapon, 2 Internal  
* \*\*Unique Trait \- *Versatile:* \*\* Once per round, you may reassign an unspent Command Token to a different officer without penalty.

### **2\. Minotaur-Class Heavy Dreadnought**

*A massive, lumbering fortress. It turns like a moon, but can withstand apocalyptic punishment.*

* **Size:** Large | **Base Hull:** 24 | **Shields:** 8 per sector | **Armor Die:** D6  
* **CT Gen:** 4 | **Base Evasion:** 3 | **Max Speed:** 1 | **RP Cost:** 150  
* **Slots:** 4 Weapon, 3 Internal  
* \*\*Unique Trait \- *Lumbering:* \*\* Speed can never be increased above 1\. Ramming an enemy deals 2D4 damage instead of 1D4.

### **3\. Zephyr-Class Strike Frigate**

*An incredibly fast glass cannon designed for flanking and hit-and-run tactics.*

* **Size:** Small | **Base Hull:** 8 | **Shields:** 3 per sector | **Armor Die:** D4  
* **CT Gen:** 4 | **Base Evasion:** 7 | **Max Speed:** 5 | **RP Cost:** 60  
* **Slots:** 2 Weapon, 1 Internal  
* \*\*Unique Trait \- *Afterburners:* \*\* "Adjust Speed" actions grant \+2 or \-2 Speed per CT spent, instead of 1\.

### **4\. Aegis-Class Escort Carrier**

*A fleet-support vessel designed to launch fighter screens and coordinate the battlefield.*

* **Size:** Large | **Base Hull:** 16 | **Shields:** 5 per sector | **Armor Die:** D4  
* **CT Gen:** 6 | **Base Evasion:** 4 | **Max Speed:** 3 | **RP Cost:** 120  
* **Slots:** 1 Weapon, 4 Internal  
* \*\*Unique Trait \- *CIC Sync:* \*\* You may spend 1 of your Command Tokens to activate an action on an Allied Player's dashboard (they suffer the Stress).

### **5\. Manticore-Class Missile Destroyer**

*A mobile artillery platform meant to sit on the backline and lob heavy ordnance.*

* **Size:** Medium | **Base Hull:** 10 | **Shields:** 4 per sector | **Armor Die:** D4  
* **CT Gen:** 5 | **Base Evasion:** 5 | **Max Speed:** 2 | **RP Cost:** 80  
* **Slots:** 3 Weapon, 1 Internal  
* \*\*Unique Trait \- *Advanced Telemetry:* \*\* Weapons fired at Long Range (5+ Hexes) do not suffer the standard \+2 TN penalty.

### **6\. Paladin-Class Heavy Cruiser**

*A frontline brawler designed to protect smaller ships and draw enemy fire.*

* **Size:** Medium | **Base Hull:** 18 | **Shields:** 6 per sector | **Armor Die:** D6  
* **CT Gen:** 5 | **Base Evasion:** 4 | **Max Speed:** 2 | **RP Cost:** 90  
* **Slots:** 3 Weapon, 2 Internal  
* \*\*Unique Trait \- *Bulwark:* \*\* Allied ships occupying a hex adjacent to the Paladin gain \+1 to their Base Evasion.

### **7\. Wraith-Class Stealth Corvette**

*An experimental electronic-warfare vessel that excels in deep-strike operations.*

* **Size:** Small | **Base Hull:** 6 | **Shields:** 2 per sector | **Armor Die:** D4  
* **CT Gen:** 4 | **Base Evasion:** 8 | **Max Speed:** 4 | **RP Cost:** 75  
* **Slots:** 2 Weapon, 2 Internal  
* \*\*Unique Trait \- *Cloaking Field:* \*\* At ranges greater than 3 Hexes, the Wraith cannot be targeted by Enemy attacks unless it fired a weapon in the previous round.

### **8\. Forge-Class Support Tender**

*A flying drydock that keeps the rest of the fleet from falling apart.*

* **Size:** Medium | **Base Hull:** 14 | **Shields:** 5 per sector | **Armor Die:** D4  
* **CT Gen:** 6 | **Base Evasion:** 4 | **Max Speed:** 2 | **RP Cost:** 85  
* **Slots:** 1 Weapon, 3 Internal  
* \*\*Unique Trait \- *Repair Drones:* \*\* Engineering "Damage Control" actions can be applied to any Allied ship within 2 hexes, rather than just yourself.
