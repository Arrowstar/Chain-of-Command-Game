# **Content Database: Command Decks**

## **1\. The Admiral's Directive Deck**

*Drawn during Phase 1\. Applies to the entire Player Fleet. Earning Fleet Favor (FF) is critical for survival.*

### **Operational Directives (Positioning & Control)**

* **Hold the Line**  
  * *Flavor:* "I don't care how hot it gets, Captain. Not one Hegemony ship crosses Sector 4."  
  * *Condition:* Ensure no enemy ships pass the rear edge of the map by the end of Round 3\.  
  * *Reward:* \+3 FF  
  * *Penalty:* \-2 FF, \+1 Stress to all Bridge Officers.  
* **No Man's Land**  
  * *Flavor:* "Keep the central corridor clear for the dreadnought's firing solution."  
  * *Condition:* No player ships may end their turn within 3 hexes of the map's center point.  
  * *Reward:* \+2 FF  
  * *Penalty:* \-1 FF

### **Tactical Directives (Combat Efficiency)**

* **Decapitation Strike**  
  * *Flavor:* "Cut off the head, and the fleet dies. Focus all fire on their flagship."  
  * *Condition:* Deal at least 6 Hull damage to the designated Enemy Command Ship this round.  
  * *Reward:* \+3 FF  
  * *Penalty:* \-1 FF  
* **Systemic Sabotage**  
  * *Flavor:* "We need that ship intact for intelligence. Disable their engines, do not destroy the hull."  
  * *Condition:* Strip the enemy's Aft Shields and deal exactly 1 Hull damage to the Aft sector.  
  * *Reward:* \+4 FF  
  * *Penalty:* \-2 FF if the ship is destroyed instead.

### **Ethical/Political Directives (Stressful Choices)**

* **Collateral Damage Acceptable**  
  * *Flavor:* "Those civilian transports are a tragic loss, but the mission comes first. Fire through them if you must."  
  * *Condition:* Destroy the enemy target, even if your line of fire passes through neutral/civilian tokens (destroying them).  
  * *Reward:* \+4 FF  
  * *Penalty:* \+3 Stress to the Captain / Tactical Officer.

## **2\. Fleet Favor (Asset) Deck**

*Purchased during Phase 2 using accumulated FF. These do not cost Command Tokens (CT) to use.*

* **Squadron Intercept (Cost: 2 FF)**  
  * *Effect:* Call in a squadron of Hegemony-defector strike fighters. Target one enemy ship within 5 hexes of any player. Roll 4x D4 attack dice that completely bypass shields.  
* **Orbital Sensor Sweep (Cost: 1 FF)**  
  * *Effect:* Reveal the next 2 "Enemy Tactic" cards from the AI deck. The Fleet Commander may rearrange them or discard one.  
* **Emergency Supply Drop (Cost: 3 FF)**  
  * *Effect:* A supply drone docks with one target Player Ship. Remove up to 2 Stress from any Officer, and immediately repair 1 Hull point.  
* **ECM Fleet Pulse (Cost: 4 FF)**  
  * *Effect:* Electronic Countermeasures. All enemy ships suffer a \-2 modifier to their Target Number (TN) calculations for this round.
