# **Content Database: Weapons & Subsystems**

## **1\. Weapon Modules**

These are slotted into the "Weapon Slots" of your chosen chassis. When Tactical takes the "Fire Weapons" action, they select one of these installed modules to construct their Volley Pool.  
*(Note: The Tactical Officer's Skill Die is always added to these pools).*

* **Mark IV Plasma Battery**  
  * **Arc:** Fore, Fore-Port, Fore-Starboard  
  * **Range:** 1-4 Hexes | **Volley Pool:** 3x D8 | **RP Cost:** 20 (Starting Default)  
  * **Effect:** Standard, reliable damage.  
* **Heavy Railgun**  
  * **Arc:** Exact Fore Face ONLY  
  * **Range:** 3-8 Hexes | **Volley Pool:** 1x D12, 1x D8 | **RP Cost:** 40  
  * **Effect:** Armor Piercing. Critical Hits with the D12 bypass Shields AND the Defender cannot roll their Armor die against them.  
* **Broadside Macrocannons**  
  * **Arc:** Aft-Port, Fore-Port, Aft-Starboard, Fore-Starboard  
  * **Range:** 1-3 Hexes | **Volley Pool:** 4x D10 | **RP Cost:** 40  
  * **Effect:** Devastating at close range, but requires complex maneuvering from Helm to line up the shot.  
* **Seeker Torpedo Tubes**  
  * **Arc:** Any  
  * **Range:** Board-wide | **Volley Pool:** 1x D20 | **RP Cost:** 35  
  * **Effect:** Fires a Torpedo Token that moves 2 hexes/round. Deals flat 4 Hull damage on hit.  
* **Point Defense Cannons (PDC)**  
  * **Arc:** Any  
  * **Range:** 1 Hex | **Volley Pool:** 4x D4 | **RP Cost:** 25  
  * **Effect:** Anti-Small Craft.
* **Ion Emitter Array**  
  * **Arc:** Fore Arc  
  * **Range:** 1-3 Hexes | **Volley Pool:** 2x D6 | **RP Cost:** 30  
  * **Effect:** Shield Breaker. Every Standard Hit removes 2 Shield points instead of 1\. However, Ion hits that overflow to the Hull deal 0 damage.  
* **Flak Artillery**  
  * **Arc:** Fore Arc  
  * **Range:** 2-5 Hexes | **Volley Pool:** 2x D8 | **RP Cost:** 35  
  * **Effect:** Area of Effect. Select a target Hex instead of a target Ship. Roll the attack against ALL units (Allied and Enemy) in that hex and all 6 adjacent hexes.

## **2\. Internal Subsystems (Active Upkeep)**

*The defector fleet operates on failing power grids. Subsystems no longer provide passive buffs; instead, equipping a subsystem adds a highly specialized Action Node to one of your Bridge Officer's stations. You must actively power them using Command Tokens during Phase 2\.*

* **Reinforced Bulkheads**  
  * *Station:* Engineering | *Cost:* 1 CT | *Stress:* 1 | **RP Cost:** 30  
  * *Action \- Polarize Plating:* For this round only, your ship's Armor Die is upgraded by one tier (e.g., D4 becomes D6).  
* **Electronic Countermeasures (ECM)**  
  * *Station:* Sensors | *Cost:* 1 CT | *Stress:* 1 | **RP Cost:** 30  
  * *Action \- Active Jamming:* Target one Enemy ship within Range 1-5. It suffers a \-2 penalty to all of its Attack Volley dice values for this round.  
* **Advanced Medical Bay**  
  * *Station:* Engineering | *Cost:* 2 CT | *Stress:* 1 | **RP Cost:** 35  
  * *Action \- Emergency Triage:* Immediately reduce the Stress of any two *other* Bridge Officers to 0\. (The Engineering officer still takes the 1 Stress for performing this action).  
* **Auto-Loader Network**  
  * *Station:* Tactical | *Cost:* 0 CT | *Stress:* 0 | **RP Cost:** 40  
  * *Effect - Passive:* Weapons with the Ordnance flag can be loaded and fired in the same round. (Without this subsystem, Ordnance weapons must be cycled and cannot be fired in the same round they are loaded).  
* **Fighter Hangar**  
  * *Station:* Tactical | *Cost:* 2 CT | *Stress:* 2 | **RP Cost:** 45  
  * *Action \- Launch Squadron:* Deploy one Allied Strike Fighter token into an adjacent hex.  
* **Auxiliary Reactor**  
  * *Station:* Engineering | *Cost:* 2 CT | *Stress:* 3 | **RP Cost:** 50  
  * *Action \- Overcharge Core:* Immediately gain 4 CT to distribute among your other stations for this round. However, the surge causes your ship to take 1 unblockable Hull damage.
